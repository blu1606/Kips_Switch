Every Vault comes with a soulbound **"Keeper Spirit"** (NFT). The state of the Vault directly affects the appearance and metadata of the Spirit.

### States
1.  **Happy/Glowing:** `lastCheckIn < 24h`
2.  **Neutral:** `24h < lastCheckIn < 7 days`
3.  **Sick/Fading:** `lastCheckIn > 7 days` (User is neglecting the vault)
4.  **Ghost (Released):** Vault expired/released.

### Mechanics
- **Feed:** `ping()` transaction = "Feed" action.
- **Streak:** Consecutive successful check-ins build a "Streak" counter in metadata.
- **Evolution:** High streak = Spirit evolves (Egg -> Baby -> Guardian).
