### Scenario A: Time expired, but Guardians say "Wait!"
- Standard Deadman's Switch relies solely on time.
- **Improvement:** When Timer = 0, Vault enters "Provisional Release" state for 48h.
- Guardians notified. They can vote to **Veto** (extend timer) if they know Owner is alive but stuck (e.g., in hospital).

### Scenario B: Owner lost keys (Social Recovery)
- Owner cannot access wallet.
- Owner asks Guardians to help.
- **M-of-N Guardians** (e.g., 3 of 5) sign a transaction to `rotate_owner_key(new_pubkey)`.
