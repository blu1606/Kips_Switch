## üèóÔ∏è The Refactor Plan

### 1. Hero Section: The "Split-Screen" Reality
Instead of a generic hero, we split the screen (or toggle) to show the two sides of the coin.

*   **Headline:** "The Switch That Triggers When You Don't."
*   **Sub-head:** "A decentralized fail-safe for your digital assets. 100% on-chain privacy."
*   **Interactive Demo (Right Side):**
    *   **State A (The Fear/Relief):** "Status: INACTIVE (30 Days)." -> **[Release Vault]** button.
    *   *Click:* Cinematic Unlock animation plays. "Funds sent to Alice." -> **Relief.**
    *   **State B (The Action):** "Don't leave it to chance." -> **[Create My Vault]** button.

### 2. Problem Section: The "Bitcoin" Hook
*   **Headline:** "Satoshi's coins are lost forever. Yours don't have to be."
*   **Visual:** The "Fading Wallet" animation (Keep existing).
*   **New Copy:** "Self-custody means if you lose access, the money burns. Deadman's Switch is the backup key you give to yourself (time-delayed)."

### 3. How It Works: The "Kip" Narrative
Simplify the technical jargon.
1.  **Lock:** Deposit assets + Encrypt Note.
2.  **Live:** Kip watches the timer. You check-in to keep him happy.
3.  **Legacy:** Timer hits zero? Kip delivers the key to your recipient.

### 4. CTA Strategy
*   **Primary:** "Start Demo (No Wallet)" -> Flash Onboarding.
*   **Secondary:** "View Contract" (For trust).
